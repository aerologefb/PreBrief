What PreBrief is

PreBrief is a pre-flight briefing tool for passengers. The checklist is operator-curated by an active line captain. Cards are advisory — they help you prepare for a flight but they don't replace airline, customs, or government instructions. Always defer to those for your specific situation.

Free tier

The free tier lets you run one active trip with the full pilot-curated checklist and notifications. No account, no card, no time limit.

PreBrief Plus subscription

  • Monthly: $4.99 per month
  • Yearly: $29.99 per year

Subscriptions auto-renew at the same price for the same period unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Your iTunes / Apple ID account is charged for renewal within 24 hours before the new period begins.

You can manage or cancel any time at Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iOS device, or inside PreBrief at Settings → Subscription → Manage subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current paid period; PreBrief Plus features remain active until then.
