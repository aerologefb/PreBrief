Brief every flight, end-to-end.

PreBrief is a pre-flight briefing for passengers, written and curated by an active A350 line captain. Tell it your flight number; it walks you from T-7d to wheels-up with the cards a pilot would actually care about.

How it works

Add your flight number. PreBrief pulls the schedule, aircraft, and route, then builds a personalised checklist that wakes up at the moments that matter — T-7d, T-72h, T-48h, T-24h, T-2h, T-30m. Each card is one short, opinionated instruction. Time-sensitive notifications fire as you approach departure so you don't miss check-in or boarding.

What's in the box

⏱

Live countdown to your departure, ticking down to the second.

📋

Smart rules — long-haul, ultra-long-range, widebody, A380 / A350 / 787, per-airline carry-on policy.

🛂

Passport-aware visa hints — visa-required, e-visa, visa-on-arrival.

🌍

Destination briefing — currency, plug type, eSIM availability, arrival weather.

Time-sensitive alerts at every T-bucket, with a one-tap "mark complete" inline action.

Privacy-first. Trips and preferences stay on your device, syncing only via your iCloud.
